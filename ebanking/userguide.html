User guide

You can register for Silkbank Direct Internet Banking by filling in the New user signup form on the Silkbank Direct Internet Banking site. On successful registration you will be sent an email instructing you to visit your Silkbank branch for activation. When you visit our branch our friendly branch staff will help you activate your internet account quickly. On account activation you will be sent an email on the email address provided by you at the time or registration which will contain your login password. At your first login into Silkbank Direct internet banking you will be asked to change your password.

Balance Inquiry

Select the Balance Inquiry on the left pane after logging in into Internet Banking and click proceed to view your updated balance.

Mini Statement

Select the Mini Statement on the left pane after logging in into Internet Banking and click proceed to view the last 8 transactions in your account

Transaction Details

Select the Transaction Details on the left pane after logging in into Internet Banking and select a date range for which you want to view the transaction details

Funds Transfer

Select the Funds Transfer on the left pane after logging in into Internet Banking and you can transfer funds from your account using your FPIN to any Silkbank account in any branch throughout Pakistan. The funds transfers are limited to Rs.250,000 per day.

Inter Bank Fund Transfer

Select the Inter Bank Fund Transfer on the left pane after logging in to Internet Banking and you can transfer funds from your account using your FPIN to any 1Link member Bank account in any branch throughout Pakistan. The funds transfers are limited to Rs. 250,000 per day.
